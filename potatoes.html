Potatoes

Description

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Potatoe masher/Electric beater
  • Preferably a hobbit to do it for you

Steps:

  1. Boil em
    1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil
    2. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm,
    3. Wait about 15 minutes
    4. drain
  2. Mash em
    1. Using a potato masher or electric beater, slowly blend milk mixture into potatoes until smooth and creamy
    2. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  3. Stick em in a stew
